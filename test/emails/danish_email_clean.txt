For random keyboard inputs, I'd recommend giving those steps a try:

1. Unplug your keyboard from your device.
3. All OmniPoint keys should be lit up yellow.
4. While the keys are yellow, please press every OmniPoint key until it has bottomed out at least once.
5. OmniPoint keys that have been fully pressed will change to a soft white color.

Hope this solves the issue! If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to ask.
